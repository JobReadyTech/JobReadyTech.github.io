TOGAF 9 Certified Certification Exams

The TOGAF 9 Practices Test


Earning your TOGAF 9 certification is one of the steps to develop your career as an enterprise architect. TOGAF 9 certification demonstrates your familiarity with the industry-standard enterprise architecture framework.

TOGAF certification is earned by passing the TOGAF Foundation (Level 1) and TOGAF Certified (Level 2) exams.

Passing your TOGAF 9 exams may seem intimidating, especially if you are just learning about TOGAF 9 certification.


What to expect in the exams?

The TOGAF® 9 exams consist of two parts. You will take both of these exams electronically, much like other graduate and professional certification exams.

The Level 1 (TOGAF Foundation) part asks 40 questions to be completed within 60 minutes. These questions are multiple choice; you have to answer 55% correctly to pass.


Level 2 (TOGAF Certified) asks eight scenario questions, to be completed within 90 minutes., Level 2 goes beyond testing basic knowledge and comprehension of TOGAF 9. It's designed to test candidates' ability to analyze and apply knowledge of the TOGAF standard.

Both TOGAF 9 exams test your ability to apply your knowledge of the TOGAF 9 documentation. Level 2 specifically asks about the best way to use TOGAF in specific scenarios.
