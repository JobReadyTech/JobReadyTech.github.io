Frequently asked questions about managing modern desktops (MD-101)

  • What is the MD-101 exam?

The MD-101 exam is for administrators who deploy, configure, protect, manage and monitor client devices and applications in an enterprise environment. Candidates manage identity, access, policies, updates and applications.

  • What should the candidate do as an administrator?

As an administrator, candidates often collaborate with Microsoft 365 Enterprise Administrator to design and implement a device strategy that meets the business needs of a modern organization.
