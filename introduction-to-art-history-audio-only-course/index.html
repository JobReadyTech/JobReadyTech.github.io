Introduction to Art History [Audio Only Course]

Have you ever heard of Warhol, Basquiat, Banksy in art history? If not, do not worry; these are some of the essential artists we cover in our free art history course. Some critical people have influenced the direction of art to create new art movements such as pop art, street art, and others. We cover various topics from art history to give you a general baseline of knowledge to help you understand art better.

The content of this course is based on our educational art history podcast. The audiogram content is a video version of the audio content from our podcast. The best part of this course is that we made it accessible to everyone. We want to teach as many people as we can about the basics of art history.

We believe that knowledge should be open and shared freely. Our goal is to make this the most understandable and fun to listen to course on Udemy.

We included almost 2 free hours of video/audio content to get you started on your journey to learning art. This is the maximum length allowed to us on this platform to provide a free and value added course. We are constantly updating the content with articles about art history. We already have over 2000 students who have joined!

Please, join us and help us build a community of people interested in learning more about art history.
