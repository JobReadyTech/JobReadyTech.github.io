Master in Agile Scrum : Complete Training & Best Practices

  • You will follow the Scrum Team’s Characteristic.

  • If you are going to play as Product Owner then what is your role, responsibility in the scrum? that you will understand here.

  • Some important terms frequently used in Scrum

  • You will learn about Time Box and its importance in Scrum

  • Scrum is an Iterative & Incremental Model. With an easy example, you will understand this in this section.
