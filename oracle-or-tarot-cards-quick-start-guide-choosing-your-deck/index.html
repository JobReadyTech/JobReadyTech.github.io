Oracle or Tarot Cards? Quick Start Guide: Choosing Your Deck

Upon completion of this course, you will be able to select the Oracle or Tarot card deck that will be your partner in giving accurate readings that flow with ease. You will know the difference between Oracle and Tarot cards and figure out which one fits your style and finally you’ll learn where to buy your new deck.

Choosing your card deck is the first step on a fun, magical and often amazing journey.

You’ll notice the course is short and each lesson is delivered in quick, easy to watch bites. I like to keep things clear and to the point so you can learn what you came for quickly, put it to use right away and get onto the fun part of selecting your deck with ease and confidence.

I know that having the right deck will feel like you have a partner in your readings, supporting you and leading you through the unfolding story. In this course I will share with you everything you need to know to select the perfect deck for where you are right now.

I have over 20 decks, mostly oracle cards and a few tarot cards. When I first started out, I got decks because they looked pretty or they were given to me and I thought I was the reason the messages I was getting were fuzzy and confusing. I was frustrating and bewildered at my inability to read the cards. It was only after I started using the information that I share with you in this course that I was able to reliably find decks that give me clear messages that come easily to me. My early decks mostly just sit around collecting dust and only get pulled out to show to other people who may resonate with them.

This course is for you if:

  • You’re just getting into reading cards and are not sure where to start

  • You’ve been reading cards for a while but aren’t getting the results you want and think it may be the deck you’re using

  • You want to know how to choose an Oracle or Tarot card deck that’s perfect for you

  • You want to know more about Oracle and Tarot cards

What you get:

  • 7 quick, to the point video lessons - packed with essential information

  • 1 review of 8 Oracle and Tarot decks - I'll share with you what I like and don't like about 8 of my decks

  • A comprehensive workbook to guide you through the lessons

  • A checklist to take with you when you go shopping for your cards

You will learn:

  • Why the right deck is important

  • How to choose the right card deck

  • About Tarot Cards

    - Major Arcana

    - Minor Arcana

    - Why you’d consider Tarot cards for your deck

  • About Oracle Cards

    - How they’re different from Tarot cards

    - Why you’d consider Tarot cards for your deck

  • How to select your deck

    - And how to know if it’s the right deck for you

  • Where to get your cards


If you are looking for your first deck or are not getting the results you want from your current cards, come and join me in this course and get the answers you need.
