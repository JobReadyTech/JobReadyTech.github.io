DAY 7 - Facebook Reach Ads

You will learn how to show your ad to as many people as possible in your target audience. Facebook reach ads are very useful for local businesses as well as e-commerce businesses launching big sales campaigns.

DAY 8 - Facebook Engagement Ads

With more than 2 billion people users on Facebook every month, advertising on the platform is a no-brainer. To ensure your ads are not overlooked though, without any action taken from those users, your ads must be engaging. Facebook post engagement is what really matters, and Facebook engagement ads are an ideal ad type to persuade users to take action.

DAY 9 - Facebook Lead Generation Ads

Lots of people want to hear from your business, but filling in forms can be difficult on mobile. Facebook lead ads makes the lead generation process easy. People can simply tap your ad and a form pops up – it's already pre-populated with their Facebook contact information and ready to be sent directly to you. With just a few taps, they can get the information they want, and you generate a qualified lead for your business.

  • Pre-populated forms means people can submit their contact information with ease, even on the small screen.

  • Facebook lead ads do most of the work of filling in contact forms, so people are more likely to complete them.

  • To get higher-quality leads, customise your forms to ask for the most important information first.

  • Lead ads can take many forms: quote or demo requests, newsletter subscriptions, event registrations and more.

DAY 10 - Facebook Traffic Ads

The Facebook traffic objective is designed to drive people to your website or app. With traffic as your objective, you can create ads that:

  • Send people to a destination such as a website, app, phone call or Messenger conversation (Website Clicks).

  • Increase the number of people going to your mobile or desktop app (App Engagement).
