Are you looking to take a Scrum Certification Exam like PSM1™ and PSPO1™, but you still feel a bit insecure or are you curious about what the questions look like? Do you want to be sure that it is aligned with the latest Scrum Guide™ 2020? Do you want to pass the exam with a high score?

The Professional Scrum Master and Professional Product Owner exams are very similar, so why not prepare and take them together or just practice more?

This unofficial course is a pack that will prepare you for both the Scrum Master and Product Owner exams with realistic practice tests so you can get familiar with the questions and receive an explanation of the answers and you will become sharp, fast, and accurate in answering scrum questions correctly.

Note: Be aware that this course does not include any videos. Only practice tests. The certification exam is not included either and must be purchased separately to correspondent institutions like scum (.) org.

*Requirements*: You need a good knowledge of the Scrum Framework to take these practice exams. You can take this course first: Agile & Scrum in Depth: Guide, Simulation and Best Practices.

Includes:

  • Exam Preparation Guide

  • Scrum cheat sheet

  • 6 Practice Tests:

    • 2 PSM1™ practice tests.

    • 2 PSPO1™ practice tests.

    • 1 test with PSM1™ and PSPO1™ questions.

    • 1 additional test a total of 160 hard questions to become sharp with your knowledge of Scrum Guide™ 2020.

Answers explained: All questions contain an explanation of the answers.

True Scrum: This course is totally aligned with the latest version of the Scrum Guide™ 2020. The explanations of the questions contain quotes from the Scrum Guide to prove that all answers must be within the rules of the Scrum Framework.
