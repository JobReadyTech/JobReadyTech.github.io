Indian Income Tax Complete Certification Course

Do you find it difficult to understand the Income Tax laws?

Or are you struggling with filing your own income tax return?

Many a time we hear people saying that they don’t understand tax, they are not aware of when tax is to be deducted (TDS), they find it difficult to apply for their own PAN number or they find it difficult to file their return of income.

So, friends to simplify all your problems and giving you an excellent clarity of all concepts, we have made this course on “Income Tax” presented by Direct and Corporate Tax expert CA Natasha Shah.

Income tax is a direct tax levied by the government on income generated by businesses and individuals. At present this law is governed by the Income Tax Act, 1961 which came into force from 1st April 1962 and there are also various Income Tax Rules, Notifications, Circulars that are also to be followed.

What will you learn?

  • Introduction and basic understanding of Income Tax

  • PAN (Permanent Account Number) – Need, uses, how to apply

  • Various types of income like Salaries, House Property, Business Income, Capital Gains, Other Sources of Income – the components and their taxability

  • Clubbing of Income & Set-off and carry forward

  • Deductions under Chapter VI-A

  • TDS – when and on what income tax is required to be deducted at the source

  • Advance tax – the installments & due dates

  • Calculation of tax liability and filing of return of income

Who can take this course?

  • Business owners

  • Accountants & Professionals

  • Graduates looking for a job

  • Any individual from Commerce / Non-Commerce background

Basically, anyone who wants to have knowledge of Income Tax and its practical aspects.

Benefits of taking this course

  • Get a comprehensive understanding of Income Tax.

  • File your own return of income or assist your tax consultant in filing the return of income of your businesses.

  • You will be able to apply for a PAN number or make corrections/changes in PAN.

  • Avoid late payment fees and interest on default by gaining knowledge of various due dates, advance tax, and TDS provisions.

  • Strengthen your resume with Income Tax knowledge.

  • Increase the job opportunities for newly passed out graduates and also for all others with knowledge of Direct tax.

On completion of this course, you will get a good understanding of the theoretical as well as the practical angle of Income Tax.
