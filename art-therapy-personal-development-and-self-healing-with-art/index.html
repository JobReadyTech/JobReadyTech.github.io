<<<<<What this course is NOT>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>

This is NOT an Art Tutorial.

I am not offering explanations of art techniques for you to become an artist. It is an inspiring course that offers Art Therapy exercises and insights into Art as Therapy to guide you during your creative process .

Furthermore he course aims to inspire you to improve your self care and work on your personal development.

Following the course is NOT the same as being in an Art therapy session. This course is by no means trying to replace your contact with a therapist in "real" life. If you have the chance to participate in offline art therapy, this should always be your priority.

My The Complete Art Therapy Course for Self Healing course "Personal Development and Self Healing through Art (Beginner) " shows you how to use art as expressive tool at home. It also introduces Art Therapy to you and hopefully inspires you to look up a professional art therapist in your area to take part in a live session.

About the instructor

Hello my name is Anike Kehinde and I have a bachelor's degree in Art Therapy-Creative Therapies since 2010.

I created Zebra Soul Art in 2019 to help aspiring Entrepreneurs build their own online Business and to support stressed out Entrepreneurs to unleash their creative potential and relax through art and self expression.

My art therapy background

After my studies I have worked in psychiatric hospitals and a pediatric center where I supported children, adolescents and adults through the use of Art therapy activities.

The therapeutic individual- or group sessions usually involved topics such as self healing, exploring their feelings and thoughts, improving self esteem, social communication skills and much more.

What impacted my Art Therapy work the most

My work as an art therapist was impacted the most by a two year project in an orphanage in the caribbean where I successfully launched Art Therapy in a school. There I helped children and teenagers in exploring and experimenting social skills and expressing thoughts and emotions through art and clay therapy.
