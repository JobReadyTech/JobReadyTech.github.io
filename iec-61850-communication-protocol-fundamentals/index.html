IEC 61850 Communication Protocol Fundamentals

IEC61850 Communication Protocol Fundamentals is a comprehensive video course where you can learn industry specific knowledge about the IEC61850 protocol standard such as that found in the utility industry.

I will teach you the basic concepts of IEC61850 standard as well as the overall IEC61850 Data Model.
